# HSM Phase 2 Landing Page — Executive Summary

## Objective
Transform the HSM landing page from a static brochure into a high-converting enrollment engine that attracts organic traffic, builds trust, and compels visitors to book a free demo class — while establishing HSM as Hyderabad's leading music school across Google, Instagram, and Facebook.

## Current State (March 2026)
- Landing page exists at https://hsm-management.vercel.app/ (also serves as admin portal entry)
- 5 sections: Hero, Alumni logos, Curriculum, Faculty teaser, Success, Footer CTA
- Trial booking modal: 6 fields, no WhatsApp option
- Instagram: 133 followers, 170 posts — content without strategy
- Google/Justdial: 4.9★ with 24 reviews — excellent rating, needs Google volume
- No teacher profiles, no testimonials, no instrument showcase, no FAQ, no location

## Key Insight
HSM's single biggest competitive advantage — **being the only Hyderabad music school offering both classical Indian (Tabla, Carnatic/Hindustani Vocals, Violin) and Western (Guitar, Drums, Piano, Keyboard) instruments** — is nowhere on the current landing page.

## What Phase 2 Delivers

### Website
11-section redesigned landing page with:
- Video hero, trust strip, instrument showcase (8 cards)
- Teacher profiles, student testimonials, class schedule overview
- FAQ, location + Google Map, WhatsApp CTA
- Simplified 3-field trial modal with WhatsApp opt-in

### SEO
- Google Business Profile fully optimised
- 25+ Google reviews within 30 days
- Local keyword targeting: "music classes Hyderabad", per-instrument terms
- LocalBusiness structured data markup

### Social Media
- Instagram: 133 → 500+ followers in 90 days (3 posts/week: Reel strategy)
- Facebook: Events, parent community group, ad retargeting
- WhatsApp Business: prospect nurture + enrolled family broadcasts
- YouTube: long-term SEO content asset (Month 2+)

### Campaigns
1. **First Note Free** — always-on trial booking driver
2. **Summer Rhythm** — June–July kids intensive camp
3. **Refer & Earn** — family referral programme
4. **Student Showcase** — quarterly public event
