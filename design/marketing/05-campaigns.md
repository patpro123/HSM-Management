# 05 — Promotional Campaigns

## Overview

Four campaigns run in parallel or sequence. Each has a distinct purpose in the funnel:

| Campaign | Funnel Stage | Timing | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| First Note Free | Acquisition | Always-on | Drive trial bookings |
| Summer Rhythm | Acquisition | June–July 2026 | Kids camp season |
| Refer & Earn | Retention + Acquisition | Month 2+ | Word-of-mouth growth |
| Student Showcase | Retention + Credibility | Quarterly | Trust, content, retention |

---

## Campaign 1: "First Note Free" (Always-On)

### Concept
The foundational offer that makes the decision to enquire zero-risk. It runs permanently across every channel and touchpoint.

### Core Message
> "Your first class is on us. No credit card. No commitment. Just music."

### Why It Works
- Removes the biggest objection: "What if it's not for us?"
- Aligns with how Indian families make educational decisions — try first, then commit
- Specific ("first class" vs vague "free trial") and honest (no hidden conditions)

### Execution

**Landing Page:**
- Hero headline: "Your first class is free"
- Trust strip: "100+ families have started this way"
- CTA: "Book Your Free Demo Now →"
- Footer CTA: "Limited demo slots available each week. Book yours."

**Google Business Profile:**
- Add "Free demo class" as a service
- Weekly Google Post: "📣 Our free first class offer is still on — [instrument] batches open. Book at [link]"

**Instagram:**
- Bio: "🎓 Your first class is FREE → link in bio"
- Monthly Reel: "This is what your child's first class at HSM looks like" (student experience walkthrough)

**WhatsApp (Broadcast to prospects):**
> "Hi [Name], just a reminder — your free demo class at HSM is still available whenever you're ready. We have [Guitar/Tabla/Vocals] slots open this week. Just reply to book! 🎵"

**Facebook Ad Creative (Month 2):**
- Headline: "First class free — no strings attached"
- Body: "8 instruments. Expert teachers. Kismatpur, Hyderabad."
- Button: "Book free class"

### Operational Notes
- Track every booking's `lead_source` in the prospects table (via UTM params or manual entry)
- Follow up every trial within 24 hours (phone call or WhatsApp)
- Script for follow-up: "Hi [Name], we're so glad [child] came in yesterday! Our [teacher name] said [child] picked it up really quickly. We'd love to have them join a regular batch — shall I send you the details?"

## Campaign 2: "Summer Rhythm" (June–July 2026)

### Concept
A structured 2-week music intensive camp for children during summer vacation. Combines multiple instruments, ends with a mini performance for parents.

### Format Options

| Format | Duration | Instruments | Price Point |
|---|---|---|---|
| Intensive Starter | 2 weeks, Mon–Sat | 1 instrument | ₹3,000 |
| Explorer Camp | 2 weeks, Mon–Sat | 2 instruments | ₹5,000 |
| Full Immersion | 4 weeks, Mon–Sat | 1 instrument | ₹5,500 |

### Core Message
> "Give your child the most musical summer of their life. 2 weeks. Expert teachers. Ends with a live performance."

### Target Audience
- Parents of children aged 6–15
- Areas: Suncity, Kismatpur, Bandlaguda, Rajendra Nagar, Attapur, Narsingi
- Channels: WhatsApp school groups, Facebook parent groups, Instagram

### Campaign Timeline

| Date | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 April | Announce on Instagram + Facebook ("Summer Rhythm is coming 🎵") |
| 15 April | Open registration (landing page section + WhatsApp link) |
| 1 May | "Only 15 seats left" urgency post on Instagram |
| 15 May | Early bird deadline (if applicable) |
| 1 June | Camp begins |
| Last day | Mini performance event — invite parents, film everything |
| Post-camp | Upload performance Reels, collect testimonials, offer regular batch enrollment |

### Channel Execution

**Instagram (April–May):**
- Countdown Reel: "Summer Rhythm camp is OPEN for registration 🥁"
- Carousel: "What your child will learn in 2 weeks of Summer Rhythm"
- Story poll: "Which instrument would your child love this summer? 🎸 🥁 🎹 🎻"
- Parent testimonial from previous year (if available) or teacher walkthrough

**Facebook:**
- Create Facebook Event: "HSM Summer Rhythm 2026"
- Post in Hyderabad parent groups (with admin permission): "Hi everyone! We're opening registrations for our summer music camp..."
- Pin event to Facebook Page

**WhatsApp:**
- Broadcast to all prospect leads: "Summer camp registration is now open! 2 weeks of music, expert teachers, ends with a live performance. Limited to 20 students. [Booking link]"
- Ask enrolled families to forward to WhatsApp school groups

**School tie-up (outreach):**
- Send a physical flyer or WhatsApp message to 5–10 nearby schools' parent representatives
- Offer a 10% group discount for 3+ students from the same school

### Post-Camp Funnel
Every Summer Rhythm student is a warm lead for regular enrollment:
1. Day before last day: "We'd love to keep [child]'s momentum going. Here's how regular batches work..."
2. Last day: Hand out enrollment brochure at the performance event
3. Day after: WhatsApp follow-up with video of child's performance + enrollment link

## Campaign 3: "Refer & Earn"

### Concept
Turn every enrolled family into an unpaid sales team. Indian families trust each other's school recommendations far more than any ad.

### Offer
> "Refer a friend who joins HSM → you get 1 month free. They get 10% off their first month."

### How It Works
1. Enrolled family shares a referral code (or just mentions HSM by name — tracked at enrollment)
2. Referred student mentions the existing family's name when booking a trial
3. If the referred student enrolls (not just trials), the referring family gets 1 month credit
4. Referred student gets 10% off first month fee

### Referral Tracking (Simple)
- In the trial booking form, add "Who referred you?" text field (already exists as `how_did_you_hear`)
- Admin marks the referring student's name in the prospects table
- When referral converts, admin notes the credit in the payments table (one month waived)

### Launch Execution

**WhatsApp Broadcast to all enrolled families:**
> "🎵 Exciting news! We've launched our Refer & Earn programme. Refer a friend whose child joins HSM → you get 1 month of classes absolutely FREE.
>
> Just ask your friend to mention your name when they book their free demo class. It's that simple!
>
> Know a family who'd love music classes? Send them this link 👇
> [trial booking link]"

**Instagram Story:**
- "Our families are our best marketers 🤍 — Refer a friend and get a free month!" (swipe up / link sticker to booking page)

**Physical card (optional):**
- Small business-card-sized referral cards given to families at the front desk:
  "Give this to a friend — they get 10% off their first month. You get a FREE month when they join!"

### Notes
- Make the credit easy to understand — one month waived from next invoice
- Remind families about it in the monthly WhatsApp newsletter
- Run a "leaderboard" (optional, fun) — share via Story: "This month's top referrer: [family name]! Thank you 🎉"

## Campaign 4: "Student Showcase" (Quarterly)

### Concept
A live student performance event held quarterly — either at the HSM premises, a rented community hall, or an outdoor amphitheatre. Open to public, families invited, prospective students welcome.

### Why This Campaign Matters
- **Retention:** Enrolled families have an event to look forward to; students have performance goals
- **Acquisition:** Prospective families see real students play — highest-trust sales tool available
- **Content:** 30 minutes of Reels, testimonials, and credibility content generated in one evening
- **Press:** Local community groups, Instagram pages, and neighbourhood news outlets will cover it if promoted right

### Event Format
```
Event: HSM Student Showcase — [Season] 2026
Duration: 90 minutes
Format:
  • 5-min welcome address
  • 6–8 student performances (1 per instrument)
  • Intermission with refreshments (keep it simple: chai + biscuits)
  • 3–4 more performances
  • Teacher performance / jam session (optional — wow moment)
  • Closing: invitation to book a free class (QR code on screen)
```

### Pre-Event Marketing (4 weeks before)

| Week | Action |
|---|---|
| Week 1 | Announce on Instagram + Facebook with date/venue |
| Week 2 | "Meet the performers" teaser Reels (each student gets a 10-sec spotlight Reel) |
| Week 3 | "Seats filling up" Stories, WhatsApp broadcast to all prospects and families |
| Week 4 | Final reminder — WhatsApp day-before reminder to confirmed attendees |

**Facebook Event:** Create and promote 4 weeks ahead. Invite all followers. Share in Hyderabad parent groups.

**WhatsApp Broadcast (to all contacts):**
> "🎵 You're invited to HSM Student Showcase — [Date]
>
> Watch our students perform live: Guitar, Tabla, Vocals, Drums and more.
>
> 📍 [Venue], Kismatpur, Hyderabad
> 🕕 [Time]
> 🎟 Free entry — bring the family!
>
> RSVP: [link or reply YES]"

### Event Day Checklist
- [ ] Filming setup: 2 phones on tripods (one wide, one close-up) for Reels content
- [ ] Branded backdrop or banner for photo ops
- [ ] QR code poster at entrance linking to trial booking page
- [ ] Feedback/testimonial form at exit (Google Form QR code)
- [ ] Photographer for still images (parent volunteer works fine)

### Post-Event (Week after)
- Upload 5–10 performance clips as Reels (spread over 2 weeks — don't dump everything at once)
- Story: "Recap of our [Season] Showcase 🎵 — thank you to everyone who came!"
- WhatsApp testimonial follow-up to all attendees who gave email/number:
  > "Hi [Name]! We loved having you at the Showcase. If you're considering enrolling, your free demo class is just one click away 👇 [link]"
- Update website testimonials section with any new quotes collected at the event
- Share event highlight video on YouTube and LinkedIn (if HSM has a company page)

### Venue Options (Budget-Conscious)
| Option | Cost | Capacity | Vibe |
|---|---|---|---|
| HSM premises | ₹0 | ~30 | Intimate, authentic |
| Nearby community hall | ₹3,000–5,000 | ~80 | Professional |
| Outdoor amphitheatre (park) | Free (permission) | 100+ | Impressive, viral-worthy |
| Café collaboration | ₹0 (split audience) | ~50 | Trendy, Instagram-friendly |

## Campaign Interlock

All four campaigns feed each other:

```
First Note Free → trial booking → enrolled student
                                        ↓
                              Student Showcase (performs)
                                        ↓
                              Refers friend → Refer & Earn
                                        ↓
                              Friend books demo → First Note Free
```

Summer Rhythm sits outside this loop but feeds directly into it — Summer Rhythm students who convert join the regular enrollment cycle.
